The Amazing Arcade Booth!

Our booth will feature the following games:

  • Space Turtles
  • Turtle Invaders
  • Shoot The Turtles!
  • Slithering Snakes: A Game
  • Pong
  • Quizzler: A Trivia Game
  • Plus, a claw machine!

There will be a barcode arcade card system with seperate accounts, each one of them containing the money added to that account and your digital game rewards (tickets)! Your money will be kept securely, and the system also makes it easier for you so you can put in money earlier, then come back and spend it. No more carrying physical tickets, all you need is your Amazing Arcade Card.


Also, every game and our Arcade Card system were programmed ourselves!

From The Children's Business Fair Website!

Whether an entrepreneur is famous like Jeff Bezos or Oprah Winfrey or they are one of the thousands of unsung business owners across this country, these are the people who make sacrifices to innovate, create jobs and serve their communities.
